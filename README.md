## Wiring Digram-

### Bluetooth - arduino 

TX to rx 

Rx to tx

Ground to ground

3.3 V to 3.3 V

____________________

### Motor driver  - arduino 

Pin 1 to 9 

Pin 2 to 10

Pin 3 to 11

Pin 4 to 12

12 v to battery 

Ground to batery Ground
